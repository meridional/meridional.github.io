Quick Sort visualization

  • Red is the pivot.

  • Blacks are the active partition.

  • Grays are the rest.

All the lines here are transformed from a pair of vanilla points: (0,0),(0,0.1) via the rotation and translatioin matrices.

Dragon Curve

There seems to be many ways to draw the dragon curve. The algorithm that I employed is due to Ben Trube.

Basic idea is:

  • Start with one line segment
  • Make one copy of the current shape and rotate the copy 90 degress about its end
  • Repeat last step until your browser crashes :)
